What is keeping you from becoming unhoused during the COVID-19 pandemic?

  • Situations can change in a moment.
  • The COVID-19 pandemic has uprooted communities, caused massive layoffs and unemployment claims, and created financial nightmares for many people and families.
  • How close are you to becoming unhoused as the pandemic continues?
  • Can you still pay your rent or mortgage if you are one of millions of Americans who lost your job due to COVID-19?
  • An estimated 50% - 78% of Americans already live paycheck to paycheck and about ⅓ of Americans have no savings.
  • An eviction crisis looms once the public health emergency and the federal eviction moratorium ends.
