Many BIPOC experience landlords refusing to rent to them due to their race, leading to housing insecurity. People without a support system are at higher risk of becoming unhoused because they cannot rely on friends and family to help them pay rent or bills or give them a place to sleep if they lose their housing. People without good health insurance or with chronic illnesses are more likely to be unable to afford their bills, including rent - especially if an illness prevents them from working. People without generational wealth (such as property, inheritance, or stocks) do not have these safeguards in place to prevent them from becoming unhoused Which of these privileges do you have?
